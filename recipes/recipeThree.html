Creamed Eggs in Corned Beef Crust

What to expect

Pie crust of white bread, flaked corned beef filling with a mushroom soup, cream and hard boiled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of corned beef
  • 1 slice of white bread, crumbed
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 6 hard cooked eggs, sliced
  • 1 can mushroom soup
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • 1 can whole mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce...the secret ingredient!

Steps

  1. Flake corned beef with fork. Add bread crumbs and eggs, mix well. Press mixture into an 8 inch plate
  2. Combine soup, milk or cream, mushrooms and the secret ingredient, Worcestershire sauce. Heat over simmering water
  3. 15 minutes before serving, bake pie crust in 350F oven for 15 minutes. Reserve a few egg slices for garnish and add remaining eggs to the sauce
  4. Remove pie from oven, pour hot creamed eggs and sauce into it and garnish with reserved egg slices and parsley. Serve immediately.
